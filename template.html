10 October 2022 (are you today's date...)

Ok pre game check. I thought i might hit the bell after FHE. Was already wearing nice pants so I brought my suit jacket. Perhaps gray on gray is a fashon crime. So what if i’m a criminal.

(Rocking the brand new shirt)

I pull up to t bizzy. They’re the fastest burrito makers in the west. (It took like 2 minutes from ordering) so i held off from ordering until being greeted by the overwhelming experience of the bell’s interior.

Entering the t bell, i saw all the regular sights, sounds and wonders. (Worker on break, soda drips all over the fountain machine, general mexican smell) but also took in the beauty of a taco bell ready for halloween. They basically have decorations everywhere and it really improves on the true mexican and day of the dead feel i’ve come to expect.

At the formal request (begging) and recommendation of multiple friends, I have given the grilled cheese burrito another chance. This is the third time: (Deferred judgment/10)

Honestly don’t think i can give the grilled cheese burrito a fair shake on this visit, despite coming exclusively to review it (and enjoy 4 gallons of baja blast). Just not feeling fantastic tonight so i’ll hold off on the official bell review™ score. Despite being made lightning fast and cracking into the burrito almost immediately, if wasn’t “beefy 5 straight off the line” hot which I appreciate. Last time i said getting the large baja blast was the flex but no. It’s buying standalone items off the menu instead of getting them in a box. The burrito was decent. I think the worst part is the grease. I don’t think burritos from t bizzy should glisten(see below). They already shine bright enough on their own✨(Without the oil from the cheese being pressed into them by a wrapping)

Baja blast: (10/10)

If i ever go into a coma just tell the doctors to put this in my IV and it’ll bring me back. They should reverse engineer this stuff and maybe they’d fix cancer.

During my visit, a worker was cleaning the tables and a manager or someone like that came out and told them to clean the window and wall behind a bench. The smell of the chemicals and talk of grease cleaning really complimented the god-tier elixir (baja blast) and the [deferred judgment] grilled cheese burrito. This has really made my taco bell experience. A true cherry on top.

This would be the end of my email but I’m waiting to see if the dood who just ordered and sat down is going to stay and eat or leave once he gets his food. Truly fascinating to observe the average taco bell enjoyer in the wild.

Ah. A true taco bell fiend. He is enjoying his t bizzy alone in the restaurant. No shame. Just taco bell love. The taco bell experience of champions. He failed to get a drink and by extension, baja blast. Looks like he got a solid order otherwise. Review of my new taco bell enjoying friend: (9/10)

6 October 2022

I pulled up and the drive thru was packed. The t bizzy was looking t busy.

Also lots of cars in the parking lot so i was scared there would actually be people besides the one worker always on break in the fine dining area.
Felt like a lady in the drive thru was looking at me and judging me but if she was, her judgment means nothing because we’re both at taco bell.

I go in, there were some people but a manageable amount.

They forgot my drink so i snagged that boy. Filled it with the great blessings of heaven (baha blast)(my cup overfloweth) and took a seat.

My last visit, they had carefully and lovingly placed the requested number of sauce packets of each variety in the box. This visit, upon opening the bag I discovered they used the handful method to count the sauces. Some might argue it’s the superior method but I withhold judgment.

the chipotle ranch grilled chicken burrito:(4/10)

decent price but not that great. Not a fan of a lukewarm burrito. A little spicy- but not the “tastes good, glad it’s there” spicy and more just annoying spicy.

the go-to beefy 5:(this visit)(6/10)

decent I guess. Could’ve been warmer. kinda just made me want more (but better) taco bell(maybe the point?) not as good as the beefy 5 i had earlier this week.

the baha blast:(Too good for my rank scheme/10)

Last but certainly not least- actually the best part. Got a large this time. It’s the same drink you get in the medium, and i’m still chilling in the lobby and will probably drink just as much either way, but there’s something special about the large- like the pride of paying 30 cents more for essentially the same thing. It makes it even more magical. When money’s tight, you gotta flex on the poors somehow.

Hit my head 0 times (so far) on this visit.


(post t bizzy update)

Got out to my car. Spilled glorious baja blast on my seat and suit. Tragic loss of baja blast. May those 4 fluid ounces rest in peace (in my 1997 rav 4 upholstery)
